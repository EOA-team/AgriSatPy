agrisatpy.operational package
=============================

The agrisatpy.operational packages allow for an operational usage (i.e., within a productive, highly automatized
environment) of AgriSatPy. The operational functions provide the baseline for creating, maintaining, updating,
querying and analyzing large amounts satellite data.

At the moment we provide support for Sentinel2-MSI in L1C and L2A processing level.
